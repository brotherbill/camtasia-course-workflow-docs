## ### 5. The Solution: A Dedicated Camtasia + Audiate Edition

A specialized edition would:

### **A. Enforce the Smart Container Doctrine**
Camtasia becomes:

- a compositor,
- a timeline container,
- a media organizer,
- an exporter.

NOT an audio editor.

### **B. Enforce the One‑Time Audiate Pass**
- Camtasia → Audiate happens once.
- All future edits occur in the `.audiate` file.
- No circular pipelines.
- No timing resets.

### **C. Remove or Hide Hazardous Features**
- ripple delete,
- destructive audio tools,
- timing‑altering operations,
- auto‑caption regeneration.

### **D. Add Guardrails**
- warnings before dangerous actions,
- locked timing by default,
- locked audio tracks,
- clear upstream/downstream authority.

### **E. Provide a Guided Workflow**
A mode specifically for:

- course creators,
- corporate trainers,
- instructional designers,
- high‑volume producers.

This mode would be:

- simple,
- safe,
- predictable,
- scalable.
